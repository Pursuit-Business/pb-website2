BAS Agent Services

ATO compliance, completely handled

As a registered BAS Agent with the Tax Practitioners Board, we take your reporting obligations off your hands - accurately, on time, and without the stress.

Your BAS is not just a form - it is a legal obligation with strict ATO deadlines, and errors can be costly. We keep your GST, PAYG withholding, and activity statements accurate and lodged on time, every quarter. You focus on running your business. We handle the ATO.

GST Reconciliation & BAS Lodgement

We reconcile your GST collected against your GST credits, prepare your Business Activity Statement, and lodge directly with the ATO on your behalf. Every figure checked, every deadline met. IAS lodgement for PAYG instalments is also included where applicable.

ABN & GST Registration

Starting out or restructuring? We handle your ABN and GST registration from end to end - making sure you are set up correctly from day one and not paying GST obligations you did not even know you had triggered.

Payroll Tax Preparation & Lodgement

If your wage bill exceeds the State Revenue Office threshold, you are liable for payroll tax. We calculate your liability, prepare the return, and lodge with the SRO - so you stay compliant at both the federal and state level without doubling your workload.

Taxable Payments Annual Report (TPAR)

Do you pay contractors? You may be required to lodge a TPAR with the ATO each year. We identify your obligations, gather the correct information, and lodge your report accurately - keeping you on the right side of ATO contractor reporting rules.

Workcover Annual Remuneration Submission

Your Workcover premium is calculated on the total remuneration paid to your workers. We prepare and submit your annual remuneration declaration accurately - so your premium is right and your workers are properly covered.

Payroll Management

Payroll that never misses a beat

Your team works hard for you. The least we can do is make sure they are paid correctly, on time, every single time - with every obligation met along the way.

Payroll is one of the most complex and high-stakes obligations a small business faces. Get it wrong and you risk ATO penalties, unhappy staff, and Fair Work headaches. We handle all of it - so you never have to lose sleep over a payroll run again.

Payroll Compliance

There is a lot more to paying people than pressing a button. We manage your TFN declarations, PAYG withholding, and superannuation obligations - and at year end, we prepare, lodge, and distribute employee payment summaries on your behalf. One less thing on your list.

Superannuation

Super is not optional, and getting it wrong is expensive. We calculate and lodge your Superannuation Guarantee contributions correctly every quarter - and from 1 July 2026, we will have you fully prepared for Payday Super, where contributions must be paid alongside every wage run.

Award Interpretation

Are you paying your team correctly under their award? Modern awards cover 90% of Australian industries and getting the rates wrong exposes you to serious risk. We interpret the relevant award for your business and ensure every employee is paid exactly what they are entitled to.

Single Touch Payroll (STP Phase 2)

We lodge your payroll data directly with the ATO through STP Phase 2 - every pay run reported in real time. Fully compliant, fully automated, and completely taken care of.

Your team deserves to be paid right. And you deserve a payroll process that just works.

Forensic Bookkeeping & BAS Rescue

Books in a mess? We fix that.

Behind on your BAS? Not sure what is even in your accounts? You are not alone - and it is exactly what we are here for.

Running a business is all-consuming. Sometimes the books get away from you - lodgements fall behind, records get messy, or you take over a business and inherit someone else's mistakes. Whatever the situation, Pursuit digs in, finds the problems, fixes them properly, and gets you back on solid ground with the ATO fast.

Forensic Bookkeeping

We go through your accounts with a fine-tooth comb - identifying errors, duplicates, missing transactions, and misclassified entries. Whether it is a year of messy records or several years of neglect, we reconstruct your books accurately and give you a clean, reliable set of accounts you can actually trust.

BAS Rescue & Catch-Up Lodgements

Overdue BAS lodgements are stressful - and the longer they sit, the worse the ATO penalties can become. We prepare and lodge all outstanding BAS returns on your behalf, communicate with the ATO where needed, and get your lodgement history completely up to date. No judgement, just solutions.

ATO Compliance Restoration

Once your books are cleaned up, we put systems in place to make sure it never happens again. Proper processes, the right cloud tools, and a dedicated Pursuit bookkeeper keeping everything on track going forward - proactively, not reactively.

Our Professional Obligations

As a registered BAS agent, we are bound by the Code of Professional Conduct under the Tax Agent Services Act 2009. This includes obligations to:

  • Act honestly and with integrity
  • Comply with all relevant taxation laws and regulations
  • Act in the best interests of our clients (unless doing so would breach the law)
  • Maintain professional competence and undertake appropriate continuing education
  • Ensure client confidentiality is maintained at all times
  • Properly supervise staff and outsourced service providers
  • Maintain appropriate administrative and quality assurance procedures

Your Responsibilities as Our Client
